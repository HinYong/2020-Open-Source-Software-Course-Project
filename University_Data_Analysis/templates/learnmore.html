University Data Analysis

    In recent years, the Ministry of Education has intensified the reform of the higher education system and promoted the construction of high-level institutions. On the basis of the 985, 211 project, put forward the "double first-class" university construction plan. As a result, the original clear distribution data of colleges and the statistics of the number of colleges are no longer accurate. Under the premise of collecting a large number of basic data, this site will clearly show the distribution data of colleges and the level data of colleges through the chart.
